A continuous smile

Ever since I was young, I was known as the kid who always smiled. I am someone who appreciates all of the good that I’ve been blessed with. When I graduated high school, I decided to study Cognitive Science (Cog Sci) at University because I saw that it gave me the tools to better understand how people thought.

I love food because of how it can connect people and of course, how delicious it is! In my free time, you will catch me watching the NBA, reviewing new boba shops, and dancing salsa.

A new realization

Halfway through my college journey, I realized that there’s another way for me to understand people, the data they generate. This was how I decided to shift my degree from Cog Sci to Data Science because I realized that it can give me the tools to quantify human choice and understand people better.

A product focused data scientist

I'm not your "typical" data scientist - I love making slide decks and giving presentations While I love using data-driven methods to help me understand users better, I also love making beautiful and engaging presentations that allow our stakeholders to get the best insights and recommendations.

A different future

We live during a time where it seems that tech is never out of sight, however, there are still so many people who struggle with fully using tech. Outside of the tech community, there are so many areas that are lacking horribly. I believe that education is the next area that needs to be revolutionized by tech. I know that great digital products can be a tool that level the playing for student everyone and tap into their max potential.
